Abstract

SocialLens structures online debates into roles and augments moderation with retrieval‑grounded evidence and transparent signals. This page presents a simplified demo of the API and UI used during research evaluations.

System Overview

Roles

Mediator synthesizes, Proponent and Opponent argue with evidence, and Fact‑Checker retrieves and cites sources.

Retrieval

Embedding search over a curated corpus (Chroma DB) provides passages and metadata for grounded reasoning.

Moderation Signals

Misinformation risk and bias cues (overgeneralization, assertion bias, us‑vs‑them framing) are surfaced with rationales.
